Fea​tures

Controller are compatible with any device&software that accepts MIDI technical standard, doesn't matter of brand or model it used with.

This means your ability to send and receive ANY type of MIDI message you need. As long as your pair device accepts MIDI, it will work perfectly and easy.

UNIVERSAL MIDI CONTROLLER

LED INDICATION

Interactive, real-time visual feedback for each footswitch with own pack of tricolored LEDs, so you always know what's going on.

Battery level, Bluetooth status, Power indicators are also ​attend.

MIDI AND FUNCTION MESSAGEs TYPES

Every control is independently programmable. Send ANY type of MIDI message including PC, CC, Note, Pitch, MMC and many more. Send ANY type of MIDI message you need!

To navigate the controller itself, use footswitch №8 to change color and current mode(bank). In different modes(banks) you may send different MIDI messages with other 7 footswitches. 1, 2, 3 modes(color per mode) are available.

MESSAGE STACKING

Send up to 20 different MIDI messages across 16 MIDI channels with each footswitch in the moment of time. This allows you to control up to 20 different devices with a single switch!

TAP-TEMPO INDICATION

Define which footswitch will act as tap-tempo switch, visually followed with LED

EXPRESSION&VOLUME PEDAL CONTROL

Connect an expression/volume pedal for smooth continuous MIDI control. Interface has 2 inputs for volume(mono type-TS 6.35mm jack) and 2 inputs for expression(stereo type-TRS ​6.35mm jack).

Expression(TRS) pedals can be used with mono(TS)-type sockets aswell.

USB CLASS COMPLIANT AND POWERED

With MIDI over USB, control your favourite DAWs, plugins and music software on your PC, Android or iOS. Easily MIDI map your desired controls to any of your footswitch, knob or socket with software like Ableton Live and Logic Pro, ar any other.

Interface is fully USB powered and has an internal Li-Ion battery, so you don't have to concern yourself with a separate power supply.

MULTI-TYPE MIDI IN/OUT

Send and receive MIDI messages via legacy MIDI 5-pin DIN, USB-C and Bluetooth wirelessly. This gives you direct connectivity to any MIDI device, without the need for a MIDI box, splitter or inerface-tointerface converters. 

MIDI RE-FORWARDING

Redirect incoming and outgoing MIDI messages from one input&output-type to another.

Need to reforward MIDI messages from Bluetooth to USB? Not an issue, at all.
This allows you to pair slave and master devices, even if they dont have same MIDI I/O's interface-types to connect

You are also able to interrupt and resume connections between I/O's and controller itself

UPDATE PANEL APPLICATION

With a powerful and intuitive editor application, programming your interface is ​fast and easy.

The Update Panel app lets you view all your switch and message settings at once, allowing you to quickly make changes and name your presets with great clarity.

You can also backup individual presets or the entire controller to your computer, so you never lose your presets and settings. Even share them with your friends if you like!​ Available as a desktop application (Windows). ​Connect with a USB-C cable.

Download and check it out!

PRESETS MANAGING

Manage your settings via app, save them as presets, store presets on a device, switch presets on-a-fly.
Upload and store up to 7 presets to the device, saving presets inside app is unlimited.
